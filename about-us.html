About us & the camp

Our intention is to help the kids forget about school for a second and enjoy their vacation. This also while discovering things about themselves, learning new skills, learning to work in a team, meeting new people and making friends, but also making new memories that they will be able to keep forever.

The team of volunteers are there to make sure that everything runs smoothly and there won't be any issues. They are also there to keep an eye on everything and everyone. They also get involved in the activities to make sure that the introverts have a good time as well and get to experience the things mentioned previously.

The kids are expected to be present at all times and be aware of a pre-established schedule. There will be plenty of activities, games and ateliers for everyone and for every taste.

Fore a whole week, we're all a group

We believe that people can be kind an d respect each other at least for a short amount of time.
We might even say that we will all be a family for the week!
We definitely encourage everyone to be kids and remember that sharing is caring and the other way around.
